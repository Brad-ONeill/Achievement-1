When I went to work with Just Eat, they were in the middle of a global rebrand. The aim of the project was to change all of the company assets along with ensuring that all automations current and future would work seemlessly with their marketing suite.

Rebranding & email journeys

The rebrand took place within Saleforce Marketing Cloud. All assets and content was stored within the platform and my role was to ensure that nothing went out incorrectly and that the automations following a customer's "journey" ran as they were supposed to. Many of the visuals have changed since that launch and I believe Just Eat have since moved off of SFMC for another host but, this remians my first experience using the platform
